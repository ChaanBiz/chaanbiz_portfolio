Gun Store Website

Year : 2022

Type of Website : E-commerce Website (Static)

Description : Non-functional website of a licensed company in the Philippines

Status : Finished

Across Gun Store is an e-commerce website of a native Filipino that wanted to establish his site, also driven by his passion for guns. The objective of this site is to create a UI-friendly website. The owner’s atmosphere revolves around white and black, with a bit of highlight of red to catch the taste of his customers.

process

  • I created a file and started coding on the spot. As the ideas on my mind became more vivid, I started to collect necessary images from the internet and selecting a color palette that I’m about to use.

  • When I had a concrete imagination of what I wanted the website would look like, I continued to code my ideas until I formed a materpiece.

    • struggles and solution

    My only struggle is when I’m making the website responsive, these logos (acted as the sponsors of the website) does not fit perfectly as I wanted them to be. They also keeps deforming everytime I adjust something which consumed my time. What I did is I set a specified width and height for these logos to be flexible in every devices.
