Creative Website

Year : 2022

Type of Website : Business Website (Static)

Description : Landing page of a real estate company aligned on techno and web development industry

Status : Finished

Creative was made by wrapped ideas out of fun. An outcome of a collaboration from two individual parties. The landing page of Creative design was focused on its design instead of building it as a dynamic website.

process

  • The first step is identifying which color pallet should be used. Since the aim is to make the website elegant, black with a gold highlight made it up.

  • Brainstorming for the website's design.

  • Preparing the needed images and logo.

  • When everything is ready, creating the template is the next step.

  • Lastly, coding the template.

    • struggles and solution

    So far, the only struggle that has been faced was the alignment of the sponsors' logos. The first try is too small, second is too big. When trying to make it responsive, it doesn't even respond. It took me a while to figure it out. In the end, customizing its width and height fixed it.
