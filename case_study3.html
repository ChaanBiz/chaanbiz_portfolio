Nourish Website

Year : 2022

Type of Website : Ecommerce Website (Dynamic)

Description : A soup business ecommerce website

Status : Not Yet Finished (Website will soon be posted)

Nourish Website is the unfinished and broadest website I've designed and developed so far. The aim is to make a responsive and dynamic website for a powerful sole proprietor who established his business internationally. The nude colors the website contain promotes a warm atmosphere which is also a goal. Its aesthetic reflects the owner's humbleness and the professionality of his business.

process

  • Based on the aim, brainstorming for the ideas that will be used is the first step. Once the type of website is identified, I'll proceed to "on-the-spot designing" on Figma to look out for a design that might suit the needed website.

  • When done, I'll gather all the images, logo, and color pallet.

  • Fixing or redesigning the website.

  • After finishing the first page's design, I'll work on the next page until it's all done.

  • When the template is concrete, I start coding.
